About

I am an Assistant Professor of Finance at the International Business School, Beijing Foreign Studies University (BFSU). My research focuses on empirical asset pricing, Chinese capital markets, and the intersection of finance and technology. I completed my postdoctoral training at Tsinghua University (2020–2022) and received my PhD in Economics from the University of International Business and Economics (2020).
